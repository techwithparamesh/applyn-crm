# Email

## Overview

Email lets you connect your work inbox (e.g. Gmail or Outlook), sync messages, and send and receive email from the CRM. You can link email threads to records (Leads, Contacts, Deals) so all communication is visible on the record detail page.

## Navigation

**Sidebar → Communication → Email**.

## UI Components

- **Inbox list** — Folders or threads; unread count and preview.
- **Thread view** — Full conversation with replies; compose and reply.
- **Composer** — To, subject, body, attachments; optional “Link to record.”
- **Link to record** — Choose module and record to attach the thread to.
- **Record emails tab** — On record detail; lists linked emails.

## User Actions

- Connect an email account (OAuth)
- Sync inbox and view threads
- Compose new emails and reply to threads
- Link an email or thread to a record
- View linked emails on the record’s Emails tab
- Track opens (if open tracking is enabled)

## Step-by-Step Guide

### How to connect your email

1. Open **Sidebar → Communication → Email**.
2. Click **Connect email** (or **Add account**).
3. Complete the OAuth flow for your provider (Gmail, Outlook, etc.).
4. After connection, the inbox syncs and appears in the Email page.

### How to link an email to a record

1. Open the **Email** thread you want to link.
2. Use **Link to record** (or the link icon) and select module (e.g. Contact) and record.
3. Save; the email appears on that record’s **Emails** tab.

### How to send an email from the CRM

1. Open **Email** and click **Compose** (or **New email**).
2. Enter recipient, subject, and body; add attachments if needed.
3. Optionally link to a record.
4. Click **Send**.

## Best Practices

- Link every important thread to the right record so you have full context later.
- Use one connected account per user to avoid confusion in shared views.
- Reply from the CRM when possible so replies stay in the same thread and stay linked.
