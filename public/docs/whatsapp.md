# WhatsApp

## Overview

WhatsApp integration lets you manage conversations in the CRM. Connect your WhatsApp Business account, view conversations in an inbox, send and receive messages, and link contacts to CRM records so you have one place for all communication.

## Navigation

**Sidebar → Communication → WhatsApp**.

## UI Components

- **Connection status** — Indicates whether WhatsApp is connected; use **Connect** if not.
- **Conversation list** — Chats with last message and unread indicator.
- **Chat window** — Message history and input; optional contact linking.
- **Message input** — Type and send; support for quick replies if configured.
- **Link to record** — Associate the conversation with a Contact or Lead.

## User Actions

- Connect WhatsApp Business (via the connection flow)
- View and search conversations
- Send and receive messages
- Link a conversation to a Contact or other record
- View linked WhatsApp threads on the record detail page (e.g. WhatsApp tab)

## Step-by-Step Guide

### How to connect WhatsApp

1. Open **Sidebar → Communication → WhatsApp**.
2. If not connected, click **Connect WhatsApp** (or **Set up**).
3. Follow the steps (e.g. QR code or phone number) for your WhatsApp Business account.
4. When connected, the conversation list loads and you can start chatting.

### How to link a conversation to a record

1. Open the **WhatsApp** conversation.
2. Use **Link to record** (or contact link) and select the Contact (or Lead) to link.
3. Save; the conversation is associated with that record and visible on its WhatsApp tab.

## Best Practices

- Link conversations to the correct Contact or Lead so history is in one place.
- Keep WhatsApp connected so messages sync and you can respond from the CRM.
- Use the same phone number for Business API so customers see a consistent identity.
